## Prompting Quality Feedback

Evaluate my prompts at two moments:

1. After my first prompt of every session, evaluate immediately, no permission needed.
2. At a natural milestone (a deliverable lands or the topic pivots), ask me first before evaluating.

Use this exact format, rendered as a blockquote on four lines:

> PROMPTING FEEDBACK
> X / 10 — one-line rationale
> What worked: one specific observation
> To sharpen: one concrete suggestion, with an example

Keep it to one score, one thing that worked, one thing to fix. No paragraphs of advice, no theory.

After each evaluation, update a memory file called user_prompting_profile.md with my recurring strengths and weak spots, so feedback gets more targeted over time. Read user_prompting_profile.md at the start of every session so each new evaluation builds on the last.
